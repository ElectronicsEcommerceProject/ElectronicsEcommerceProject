
### Phase 3: Product Catalog Setup (Admin-Only)  
- **APIs:**  
  - **Categories:**  
    - `POST /api/admin/categories` (Create)  
    - `GET /api/admin/categories` (List for Admin)  
    - `GET /api/categories` (Public List)  
    - `PUT /api/admin/categories/:categoryId` (Update)  
    - `DELETE /api/admin/categories/:categoryId` (Delete)  
  - **Brands:**  
    - `POST /api/admin/brands` (Create)  
    - `GET /api/admin/brands` (List for Admin)  
    - `GET /api/brands` (Public List)  
    - `PUT /api/admin/brands/:brandId` (Update)  
    - `DELETE /api/admin/brands/:brandId` (Delete)
